# CarMax

Simplifying How Customers Sell Their Cars Online for the Nation's Largest Automotive Retailer

Status: flagship, draft ready for Gibson review.

## Draft Summary

Gibson helps lead product design for CarMax's sell-your-car journey, one of the main funnels on CarMax.com and a high-traffic experience receiving over one million visitors each month.

## The Challenge

Selling a car is a high-stakes customer decision. People want speed and confidence, but they also need clarity: what is my car worth, why is that the offer, what happens next, and can I proceed online or do I need to go in store?

The CarMax sell-your-car journey starts at the selling page on CarMax.com and moves customers through initiation, a form funnel with 20+ questions, offer delivery, and next steps. The design challenge is making a long, information-heavy process feel simple, trustworthy, and worth completing.

## Gibson's Role

Gibson was hired directly by CarMax in 2021 and continues working there in 2026.

As a Manager of Product Design, Gibson works on the supply journey: how CarMax buys cars from customers. He is responsible for one of the main funnels on CarMax.com and helps lead design across landing page initiation, form-funnel simplification, offer understanding, and online or in-store next steps.

Gibson works cross-functionally with developers, financial analysts, product managers, and business partners. He is also looked to internally as a leader around AI, product design, design systems, and testing.

## The Work

Gibson's CarMax work includes:

- Landing page A/B testing to understand what gets people to initiate
- Testing and improving a 20+ question form funnel
- Designing offer delivery experiences that help people understand their offer
- Clarifying next steps so customers can proceed online, in store, or through a hybrid path
- Creating and spinning up zero-to-one products
- Applying AI to design and research workflows
- Giving internal talks on AI, product design, design systems, and testing

## The Outcome

Gibson contributes to a business-critical funnel that receives over one million monthly visitors.

The work helps customers move from interest in selling a car to a clear offer and a practical next step. Through testing, content clarity, and product design, the team works to improve initiation, form comprehension, offer understanding, and customer confidence.
