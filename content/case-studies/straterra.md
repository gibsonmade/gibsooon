# Straterra

How Gibson Helped a Commercial Property Platform Move From Idea to Launch

Status: flagship, draft ready for Gibson review.

## Draft Summary

Gibson helped a serial entrepreneur turn a commercial-property platform concept into a launch-ready brand, website, lead-generation flow, and product experience.

## The Challenge

The client was a serial entrepreneur with a business concept that needed to move out of his head and into a launch-ready state.

The product vision combined a B2B SaaS-like public website with a private commercial-property listings platform. Companies needed to understand the platform, inquire to join, and eventually access a member-only experience where commercial property opportunities could be explored through advisory, investment, or syndication participation.

The challenge was not just to make a website. It was to create the strategic clarity, name, brand, visual direction, lead-generation path, and product UX needed to make the business feel real enough to sell.

## Gibson's Role

Gibson was hired as a freelance strategy, brand, UX/UI, and digital product partner in 2025-2026 and continues advising.

He worked directly with the founder to translate the business concept, guide naming, shape the brand, provide creative direction, design the website, and create UX/UI for the core platform experience.

## The Work

Gibson helped create the launch foundation:

- Concept translation and launch planning
- Naming exercise
- Surveys and generated reports to inform naming decisions
- Brand guidelines
- Type and style guidance
- Art direction and creative direction
- B2B SaaS-like website
- Inquiry and lead-generation form
- Platform UX/UI
- Commercial-property listings experience
- Ongoing advising

## The Outcome

The client moved from idea to a launch-ready product and website.

The public-facing website gave the platform a professional presence. The lead-generation flow helped the business prospect, target, and sign up interested companies. The private platform experience created a working foundation for commercial-property listings and participation opportunities.

Some proof points, including 100+ Houston listings, need final approval before publication.
