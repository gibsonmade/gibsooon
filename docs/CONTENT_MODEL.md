## Inquiry Submission

Seven-question v1:

1. What are you trying to build or change?
2. What is happening in the business that makes this important now?
3. What kind of help do you need: strategy, brand, website, product UX, AI systems, launch support, or the full system?
4. What already exists: brand, content, website, product, CMS, audience, team, or technical setup?
5. What does a successful project need to accomplish?
6. What budget range are you considering?
7. What timeline are you hoping for?

Also collect name, email, company, website, and optional notes.
