## Professional Story Draft

Gibson's career sits at the intersection of product strategy, UX leadership, brand systems, digital product design, and launch execution.

The through-line is turning complex ideas into usable systems:

- At Mini Murals, Gibson helped a civic art idea become a brand, website, map experience, and campaign system.
- At The Home Depot, Gibson led UX designers and researchers working on reusable project-shopping patterns for one of the largest retail catalogs in the country.
- At CarMax, Gibson helps lead product design for the sell-your-car journey, a high-traffic funnel that blends customer empathy, testing, offer comprehension, and AI-driven design/research practices.
- Through freelance work like Straterra, Gibson helps founders move raw business concepts into named brands, websites, lead-generation flows, and product UX.

The About page should make Gibson feel credible first, then creative, then personal.
