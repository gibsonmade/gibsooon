# Project North Star

## Site

Gibson Hall portfolio website.

## Purpose

Help founders and growing businesses hire Gibson for end-to-end digital transformation projects.

Visitors should think:

1. Gibson is smart.
2. Gibson can help me.

The site should communicate trust, intelligence, and creativity, in that order.

## Positioning

Gibson Hall helps founders turn ideas into reality through beautiful, strategic digital experiences.

Gibson works soup-to-nuts across strategy, brand, logo, content, custom visuals, website design, website build, CMS, AI systems, motion, and launch.

## Primary Offer

End-to-End Digital Transformation.

Ideal engagement: `$10,000+`.

A larger engagement may include brand strategy, logo refresh or logo from scratch, website, custom icons, custom photography or AI-generated photography, custom graphics, motion animation, responsive design, CMS, and launch support.

## Secondary Offers

- Product strategy
- UX strategy
- Website strategy
- Digital consulting
- AI and automation consulting

## Audience

Founder-led businesses, small teams, growing companies, agencies, and operators.

Typical fit:

- Profitable and growing
- Under 50 employees, often under 20
- Able to invest `$10k+` into a serious digital project

## Quietly Repel

- Logo-only projects
- Tiny one-off projects
- Banner ads
- Cheap freelance requests
- Maintenance-only work
- Unclear low-budget work
- Production help with no strategy

## Primary Conversion

Discovery questionnaire with seven thoughtful questions. V1 sends inquiry details to Gibson by email.
