# Case Study Claims Review

This file tracks what is currently safe, what needs Gibson approval, and what should not be published yet.

## Global Rules

- Do not publish unsupported conversion, revenue, or performance metrics.
- Do not publish private member-only platform screens.
- Do not publish internal tools, proprietary research artifacts, or confidential A/B test details without approval.
- Prefer abstracted or recreated visuals when public screenshots are unclear.
- Keep collaboration language accurate: Gibson helped, led, managed, partnered, or contributed based on the actual role.

- Proprietary screenshots without approval.
