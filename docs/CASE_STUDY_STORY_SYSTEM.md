# Case Study Story System

This file converts intake notes into reusable story guidance for the flagship case studies.

## Role Summaries

### The Home Depot

Gibson was Senior Manager of UX Design from 2017 to 2021, leading product designers and UX researchers inside The Home Depot. He helped teams create reusable, validated UX patterns for project-based shopping experiences across HomeDepot.com while partnering with developers, business owners, analysts, and product teams.

### CarMax

Gibson is a Manager of Product Design at CarMax from 2021 through 2026, working on the sell-your-car / supply journey. He helps lead design for a high-traffic funnel spanning landing page initiation, a 20+ question form flow, offer delivery, and online or in-store next steps.

### Mini Murals

Gibson served as CTO from approximately 2012 to 2016, helping the CEO and COO turn a Houston public-art initiative into a brand, website, interactive map, and campaign system. His work supported discovery by neighborhood, artist, and theme.

### Straterra

Gibson was hired as a freelance strategy, brand, UX/UI, and digital product partner from 2025 to 2026 and continues advising. He helped a serial entrepreneur move a commercial-property platform concept into a launch-ready brand, website, lead-generation flow, and product UX.

## Constraints

### The Home Depot

- Massive retail catalog.
- Highly customizable home improvement projects.
- Need for reusable patterns across teams.
- Mobile and omnichannel considerations.
- Design system consistency.
- No unapproved performance claims.

### CarMax

- High-traffic, business-critical funnel.
- High-stakes customer decision.
- 20+ question form flow.
- Complex offer comprehension.
- Online and in-store paths.
- No unapproved conversion, revenue, or internal test details.

### Mini Murals

- Distributed public artwork across Houston.
- Multiple artists, neighborhoods, districts, and civic partners.
- Need for public discovery by neighborhood, artist, and theme.
- Early-stage organization and campaign needs.
- Award wording needs final confirmation.

### Straterra

- Raw founder concept.
- Need to create credibility before the business was fully known.
- Public website plus private member-only platform.
- Confidential property and listing data.
- Category naming and exact brand spelling need confirmation.

## Transformation Arcs

### The Home Depot

Before: customers had to translate complex home projects into individual product choices themselves.

During: Gibson helped teams create reusable, validated patterns for project calculators, configurators, inputs, layouts, and mobile flows.

After: project-shopping experiences could better guide customers toward complete material lists and omnichannel purchase paths.

### CarMax

Before: selling a car online required customers to move through a high-stakes flow with many questions and trust decisions.

During: Gibson helped design, test, and improve the landing page, 20+ question form flow, offer delivery, and next-step clarity.

After: customers could move from sell intent to offer understanding and online or in-store next steps with more clarity.

### Mini Murals

Before: murals existed across Houston, but discovery depended heavily on physical location and scattered awareness.

During: Gibson helped create the brand, website, map experience, campaigns, and operational systems.

After: hundreds of murals became easier to browse by neighborhood, artist, and theme.

### Straterra

Before: the client had a business idea that needed definition, naming, brand structure, a website, and product UX.

During: Gibson helped with naming, research, brand guidelines, art direction, lead generation, public website design, and platform UX/UI.

After: the concept became a more launch-ready commercial-property platform with a professional public presence and working product foundation.
